About Pro Risk

The Pro Risk indicator is a composite metric that combines multiple on-chain signals to provide a single risk assessment score from 0 to 100. Components include:

  • MVRV-Z Score - Market valuation vs realized valuation
  • NUPL - Net unrealized profit/loss
  • SOPR - Spent output profit ratio
  • Reserve Risk - HODL confidence indicator
  • Puell Multiple - Miner revenue health
