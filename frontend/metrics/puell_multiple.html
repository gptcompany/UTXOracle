About Puell Multiple

The Puell Multiple measures the ratio of daily coin issuance value (in USD) to the 365-day moving average of daily issuance value. It indicates miner profitability relative to historical norms.

  • Low Values (<0.5): Miners are earning below average - potential capitulation, good accumulation opportunity
  • High Values (>4): Miners are earning significantly above average - increased sell pressure likely
