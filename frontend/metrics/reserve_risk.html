About Reserve Risk

Reserve Risk assesses the risk/reward of investing in Bitcoin at any given point by comparing the price to long-term holder confidence (HODL Bank).

  • Low Reserve Risk: High confidence among HODLers, price may be undervalued
  • High Reserve Risk: Low confidence, often coincides with cycle tops

Formula: Reserve Risk = Price / (HODL Bank)
